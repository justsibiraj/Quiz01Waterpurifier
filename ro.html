💧 RO Purifier Recommended

Your water source or taste suggests high TDS (Total Dissolved Solids) — likely from borewell or mixed sources. This means your water contains excess salts, heavy metals, or hardness that can’t be removed by simple filters.

Why RO: Reverse Osmosis pushes water through a semi-permeable membrane that removes up to 95–99% of dissolved impurities, making it safe and better-tasting.

Tips:

  • Pick an RO with a TDS controller or mineralizer to retain essential minerals.
  • RO requires electricity and regular maintenance (₹300–₹600/month typical).
  • Consider RO + UV combo if you suspect microbial contamination.
