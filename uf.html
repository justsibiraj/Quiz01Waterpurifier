✅ UF or Gravity Purifier Recommended

Your water supply seems to be low in dissolved solids (likely municipal water), clear-tasting, and relatively safe.

Why UF: Ultrafiltration uses a membrane to remove physical impurities, bacteria, and cysts — without removing minerals. It works without electricity and costs less to maintain.

When to avoid RO: RO is only needed for high TDS (>500–600 ppm). Using it on clean municipal water wastes water and money.

Tips:

  • Replace filters annually (low cost).
  • Great option during power cuts or in apartments with clear tap water.
